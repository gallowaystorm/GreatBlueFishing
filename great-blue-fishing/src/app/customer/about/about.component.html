Who is Great Blue Fishing Inc.?

History

Great Blue Fishing Inc. was founded by Garrett Wiles in 2019. His passion for fishing resonated from his youth where he would spend summers at his river house in Urbanna, Virginia. The inspiration for this company streams from that love of fishing and his want to preserve the sport for generations to come.

Purpose

The purpose of this organzation is to support local ecosystems through donations made from a portion of proceeds. At the same time, we at Great Blue want to inspire others to pursue fishing and other outdoor activites. Each charity that we support has a special place in our heart, making it fullfilling to provide a platform to give back.

Community

Fishing and ecosystem preservation aren't the only things on our mind. We also try to incorporate our philosphy into the daily lives of our neighbors. To accomplish this, we buy local, support community events, and do our best to make others feel welcomed.
